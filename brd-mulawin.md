# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

**Project:** Barangay Mulawin Website — Official Community Web Portal  
**Date:** 2026-05-25  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Barangay Mulawin LGU  
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The Barangay Mulawin Official Website is a public-facing web portal for residents of Barangay Mulawin, Rodriguez (Montalban), Rizal. It provides residents with centralized access to barangay services information, community announcements, official documents requirements, and contact details — reducing the need for physical visits to the barangay hall for routine inquiries.

---

## 2. The Problem & Opportunity

**The Problem:**

Residents of Barangay Mulawin currently have no central digital resource for barangay information. Inquiries about document requirements, office hours, community programs, and official contact details require physical visits or informal word-of-mouth — causing wasted trips, delays, and frustration, especially for working residents and those living farther from the barangay hall.

**The Opportunity:**

Smartphone and internet penetration in Rodriguez has grown significantly. A mobile-first web portal can serve the ~12,500 residents of Barangay Mulawin by making information available 24/7 — reducing foot traffic, improving trust in local government, and enabling digital-first communication.

**Target Customer / User:**

A resident of Barangay Mulawin aged 20–55, who uses a smartphone daily, needs to request a barangay clearance or certificate, and wants to check requirements before visiting — currently frustrated by uncertainty about what to bring and whether the hall is open.

---

## 3. Strategic Alignment

**Primary Goal (0–12 months):** Reduce the average number of "unnecessary visits" (visits where residents were turned away due to incomplete documents) by 50% by providing clear, accessible service requirement information online.

**Secondary Goals:**
- Build resident trust in the barangay through transparent, up-to-date online presence.
- Establish a communications channel (news & announcements) to supplement the Facebook page.
- Provide a digital record of barangay officials, history, and programs.

---

## 4. Scope

**In Scope:**

- Public-facing website accessible on mobile and desktop browsers
- Home page with hero, announcements, services overview, officials preview
- About page: history, mission/vision, core values, timeline
- Officials page: Punong Barangay, Kagawads, SK, appointed officials
- Services page: all document types with requirements, fees, processing times
- News & Announcements page: latest community updates and events
- Contact page: contact form, office hours, address, embedded map
- Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- SEO metadata for all pages

**Out of Scope (V1):**

- Online appointment or document request submission
- User accounts / login / authentication
- Online payment for barangay fees
- Real-time chat support
- Integration with national e-government systems (eGov PH)
- Push notification / SMS alerts
- Admin CMS (content management system) — content is static for V1
